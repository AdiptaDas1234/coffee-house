Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart in downtown Seattle. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, had a passion for bringing exceptional coffee to her community.

Today, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood coffee house while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability. We source our beans directly from ethical farms and roast them in-house to bring you the freshest flavors.

At Brew Haven, we believe coffee is more than a drink—it's an experience, a ritual, and a way to bring people together.
